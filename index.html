CHEAT, LIE, DENY

I'm currently developing CHEAT, LIE, DENY, a social deception party game designed for groups in real-life settings. Players join a shared lobby and take part in various game modes where lying, bluffing, and deduction are key. In one mode, all but one player receive a question—the odd one out must blend in without getting caught. The game focuses on quick rounds, group interaction, and face-to-face gameplay, making it ideal for parties and casual gatherings.
